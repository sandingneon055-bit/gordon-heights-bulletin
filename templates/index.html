Welcome to the Barangay Gordon Heights Official Bulletin and Information System. By accessing or using this system, you agree to be bound by the following Terms and Conditions. Please read them carefully before registering or logging in.

By creating an account or using any feature of this system, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agreed to these Terms and Conditions. If you do not agree, please do not use this system.

2. Purpose of the System

The Barangay Gordon Heights Bulletin System is an official digital platform designed to:

  • Disseminate official barangay announcements and information
  • Provide emergency alerts and IoT-based community safety notifications
  • Allow residents and visitors to report community issues and incidents
  • Display emergency hotlines and event schedules

This system is operated by the Office of the Barangay of Gordon Heights, Olongapo City, Zambales, Philippines.

3. User Accounts and Registration

3.1 Citizens / Residents — Residents of Barangay Gordon Heights may register using their full name, email address, block/lot/street address, and contact number. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your account credentials.

3.2 Non-Citizens / Visitors — Visitors or non-residents may register with limited access. Non-citizen accounts are restricted to submitting photo incident reports, viewing emergency alerts, and accessing hotlines.

3.3 Accuracy of Information — You agree to provide accurate, current, and complete information during registration. Providing false information may result in account deactivation.

3.4 Account Security — You are responsible for all activities that occur under your account. Notify the Barangay Admin immediately of any unauthorized use.

4. Acceptable Use Policy

You agree NOT to use this system to:

  • Submit false, misleading, or fraudulent reports or information
  • Upload inappropriate, offensive, or illegal content
  • Impersonate any person or barangay official
  • Attempt to gain unauthorized access to the system or other user accounts
  • Use the system for any commercial or unauthorized purpose
  • Harass, threaten, or harm other users or barangay officials

Violation of these terms may result in immediate account suspension and may be reported to the appropriate authorities.

5. Photo and Incident Reports

5.1 When submitting a photo report, your image, GPS location, date, and time will be automatically captured and sent to the Barangay Admin.

5.2 By submitting a report, you confirm that the information and photo are truthful and accurately represent the incident you witnessed.

5.3 Submitting false incident reports is a violation of these terms and may constitute a criminal offense under Philippine law.

5.4 The Barangay reserves the right to act on, archive, or remove submitted reports at its discretion.

6. Privacy and Data Collection

6.1 This system collects personal information including your name, email, address, contact number, and location data solely for the purpose of providing barangay services.

6.2 Your information will not be sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties without your consent, except as required by law or by order of a competent authority.

6.3 Location data captured during incident reports is used solely to identify the location of the reported incident.

6.4 This system complies with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173) of the Philippines.

7. Emergency Alerts and IoT Sensors

7.1 Emergency alerts are issued by authorized Barangay Officials only. These alerts are provided for informational and safety purposes.

7.2 IoT sensor data displayed in this system is intended to support community safety. The Barangay does not guarantee the 100% accuracy of sensor readings at all times.

7.3 In the event of a genuine emergency, always follow the instructions of authorized emergency personnel.
